Leveraging Performance

Our innovative company merges cutting-edge exercise science with the art of athletic performance. By fusing the principles of physiology, biomechanics, psychology, and nutrition, we deliver a comprehensive approach that maximizes the potential of lacrosse athletes. From enhancing cardiovascular endurance and optimizing movement mechanics to honing in game environment decision making and nutrition to fuel performance, our tailored programs empower athletes frequently reach new heights on the field. With our unwavering commitment to evidence-based training methodologies, we pave the way for a new era of lacrosse excellence, revolutionizing the way athletes train and perform in this exhilarating sport.

Services

Strength & Conditioning

Our formula targets functional strength, speed, force generation, and endurance to optimize on-field performance. Detailed workout plans that tailored to translate to the game.

Speed & Agility

Maximize movement patterns and improve coordination in a dynamic environment. Add new aspects to your game with live repititions.

Programming & Review

Long-term success is accomplished through consistent effort, planning, and performance analysis. Program packets are available for Strength & Conditioning, Speed & Agility, Nutrition, and film review.

Nutrition

Nutrition plays a vital role in determining your athletic potential. Proper fueling and nourishment provide the essential nutrients needed for optimal performance, endurance, and recovery. By making smart dietary choices, you can unlock your full athletic capabilities and maximize your overall potential on and off the field.

Long Stick Midfield Physical Readiness & Transition Presence

LSM and all positions require a unique blend of physical readiness and transition presence. These versatile athletes serve as a crucial link between the defensive and offensive units, playing a pivotal role in disrupting opposing offenses while being a transition threat.

Team defense & Overall IQ

Team defense in any sport requires a high level of coordination, communication, and collective understanding. Players must train not only their individual skills but also develop a comprehensive defensive IQ to excel as a cohesive unit, a necessary element of all well-rounded athletes looking to get to the next level.

Division I Offensive Lacrosse IQ and Stick Work

Having a high lacrosse IQ and exceptional stick work skills are paramount for competing at any level. Lacrosse IQ encompasses tactical understanding, field awareness, the ability to make quick, strategic decisions, enables athletes effectively analyze in-game environments, exploit defensive weaknesses, and create scoring opportunities.

Dyanmic Team offense and Individual Development

Team offense and dynamic development are fundamental elements in lacrosse, driving the success of a team. A well-coordinated offense involves strategic positioning, effective passing, and seamless communication to generate scoring chances. Embracing dynamic development ensures continuous improvement, innovation, and the ability to adapt to changing game situations, giving lacrosse teams a competitive advantage.
