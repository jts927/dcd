Jack Simon

Senior Instructor

Jack Simon taking a shot against Duke at Konskinen Stadium. Feburary 2nd, 2023.

Bio

Jack Simon is a student-athlete from Westchester, New York where he played both LSM and close defenese for the Westlake Wildcats (Thornwood, NY). Commiting in the summer of 2018 to High Point University, where he would continue on to graduate in the spring of May 2023 earning a major Bachelor of Science in Exercise Science (Kinesiology) and minor in Computer Science. He intends to return to HPU for a graduate year where he will be earning a Master's in Business Administration with a focus in Data Analytics.

Jack has a passion for the game of lacrosse and developing student-athletes to their greatest potential. With years of highly-competitive experience and great mentors he has decided to pass on knowledge for the better developement of the future athletes.
